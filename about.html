About

Surwa Community and Education Development Initiative (SCEDI) is founded in 2020 and registered with Corporate Affairs Commission in 2024 as a local non-government and non-profit organization to promote community and education development through collaborative partnerships with local communities and government by employing innovative approaches and self-help initiatives to create a sustainable impact.

Our Vision

To create a community with collective commitment that takes ownership and sustains all developmental projects for lifelong impact through cost-effective and affordable alternatives.

Our Core Values

These principles guide our work and decision-making process.

Integrity

We are committed to honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all our operations.

Inclusivity

We value diversity and ensure our programs are accessible to all members of the community.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions and continuously adapt to meet the evolving needs of our community.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and working together to achieve greater impact.
