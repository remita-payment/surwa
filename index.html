Our Story

Surwa Community and Education Development Initiative (SCEDI) is founded in 2020 and registered with Corporate Affairs Commission in 2024 as a local non-government and non-profit organization to promote community and education development through collaborative partnerships with local communities and government by employing innovative approaches and self-help initiatives to create a sustainable impact.
